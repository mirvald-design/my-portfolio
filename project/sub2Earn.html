About

Sub2Earn - A platform that transforms ordinary social subscriptions into an income earning opportunity.

Task

The client came in with the challenge of creating a platform that would attract user interest in social account subscriptions and also provide account owners with the means to monetize them.

Problem

Many users experience low motivation to sign up for new social accounts, and account owners face limited opportunities to monetize their platforms.

Decision

1- Authorization We have developed a simple registration system via Google accounts or phone numbers specifically for Influencers.

2. Creating Campaigns: For Influencers, we have provided the ability to create personalized campaigns with set rewards for subscriptions. Campaigns can be either public or private.

3. Statistics Management: Influencers can monitor the statistics of their campaigns, evaluate their performance and make adjustments to best engage audiences.

4. Subscriber Participation: Regular users can register, add their social accounts and participate in the Influencer campaigns, receiving cash rewards for subscribing.

5. Security and Trust: We have ensured maximum security and data privacy to ensure that every participant on the platform feel confident.

Conclusion

The result of our work was the Sub2Earn platform, which turned ordinary subscriptions into valuable assets. Influencers were able to monetize their accounts, expand their audience, and easily to manage campaigns. Ordinary users began receiving real rewards for their activity, creating a mutually beneficial interaction.
